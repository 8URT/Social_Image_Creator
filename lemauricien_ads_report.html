Ad Format Descriptions
Display (Manual): Traditional banner-style advertisements placed within webpage content.
Vignette: Full-screen overlay ads that appear between content pages.
Anchor: Sticky ads that follow users as they scroll down the page.
Multiplex (Manual): Multiple ads displayed together in a single unit, allowing for more ad space in a compact area.
In-page: Ads embedded directly within the content of the webpage, blending with the natural flow of articles.
Display (Auto): Display ads automatically placed by Google's system based on optimal positioning algorithms.
Multiplex (Auto): Multiple ads automatically placed by Google's system in a single unit.

RPM Performance & Viewability Rates by Ad Format
